Clear Sky

Clear Sky is more than just a game project; it is the culmination of a long-term creative journey. At its heart is a character I created years ago, now finally brought to life through a blend of engineering logic and digital artistry.

🎨 A One-Man Studio

In this repository, the "System Engineer" and the "CG Artist" work together. This project allowed me to manage the full creative and technical pipeline:

    The Vision: Bringing a legacy character into a playable 2D environment.

    The Craft: Hand-drawn 2D art and animations designed to capture a specific mood and flow.

    The Sound: An original soundtrack and sound effects composed to immerse the player in the world of Clear Sky.

    The Logic: Using Unity and C# to build a responsive, modular framework that feels as fluid as the art looks.

🛠 Built With

    Engine: Unity 6.3

    Language: C#

    Art & Animation: Photoshop & Creative Suite

    Audio: Original compositions

✨ Why This Matters

This project is a testament to the "Quick Learner" mindset. It’s about the challenge of mastering new tools—whether it’s refining a game loop or mixing a music track—to ensure that the final result is a cohesive, polished experience. It’s a showcase of what happens when analytical rigor meets creative passion.
